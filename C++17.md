New features in C++17.

Language:
- Guaranteed [[Copy Elision]] and [[Move Elision]].
- [[constexpr]] [[Lambda Expression]]s.
- [[Class Template Argument Deduction]]
- [[Fold Expressions]]
- [[Structured Bindings]]
- [[Nested Namespace]]
- [[If-Init Expression]]
- [[If-Constexpr]]
- [[Auto Non-Type Template Parameters]]
- [[Inline Variable]]
- [[Static Assert]]
- [[__has_include]]

Library:

- More [[constexpr]] in the [[Standard Library]].
- [[String View]].
